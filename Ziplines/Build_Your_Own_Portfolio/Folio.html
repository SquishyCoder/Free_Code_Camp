About Me

I am a self-starter who consistently takes positive initiatives to improve my skills and be a valuable team member. My ability to adapt to various, often challenging, environments and eagerness to work in web development are among the strongest assets I have to contribute.

I take great strides (and a bit of pride) in learning the ins and outs of my positions quickly resulting in advancing to more demanding responsibilities such as working with the principals of The Green Economy to develop website and social media strategies, or becoming the Editorial lead to set up and manage clients' call and web tracking campaigns for ThomasNet. Attention to detail, solid prioritizing and organizational abilities, and personal accessibility to clients, sales representatives and departmental staff during my time at ThomasNet were critical.

In case you are wondering why an octopus other than it is my spirit animal, I am very experienced in switching tasks at a drop of the hat or even handling multiple responsibilities simultaneously in an efficient and timely manner. I made continuous efforts to go beyond my job description by being proactive in finding solutions to various issues while completing all general tasks on tight deadlines.

I love collaborating and being a part of a great team, but I am pretty solid at solo gigs as well. If you would like to Get In Touch (G.I.T), please feel free to contact me!
